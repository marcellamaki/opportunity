Submit a Problem Statement for 2019 Sprints


Interested in participating?

We’re currently inviting federal agencies to submit ideas for 2019 sprint topics! Each year, our 14-week technology development sprints focus on key challenges such as the opioid crisis, disaster preparedness, STEM education, and more, that are identified by agencies across government.

To submit a topic:

  1. Download our guidelines for developing problem statements representing new or perennial challenges to tackle in this year’s sprints.
  2. Complete the template and send it back to us at census.opportunityproject@census.gov.
  3. Once we select the problem statements, tech teams, user advocates and product advisors will join the sprint and prototype solutions to those challenges alongside federal agencies.

The deadline for submissions is April 30, 2019!
